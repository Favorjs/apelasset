How do I view my Account Statement for a particular period?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Investments tab, click Account
3. Specify the Start Date of the account statement
4. Specify the End Date of the account statement
5. Click OK

How do I view my Portfolio Holdings?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Investments tab, click Portfolio Holdings

How do I view my Trade History?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Trade tab, click Trade History
3. Select Open, Rejected or Cancelled orders to view their respective history

How do I view Purchase Contract Notes?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Trade tab, click Purchase Contract Notes
3. Specify the Start Date
4. Specify the End Date
5. Click OK

How do I view my Portfolio Transactions?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Trade tab, click Portfolio Transactions

How do I view my Certificates?

1. Login to your Apel Asset Limited account on the online portal
2. On the Investments tab, click Certificates
3. Specify the Start Date
4. Specify the End Date
5. Click OK
