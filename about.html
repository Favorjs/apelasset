HISTORY

Apel Capital Registrars Limited “ACRL”, a subsidiary of Apel group and one of the leading Registrars companies in Nigeria was recently registered as a separate legal entity from Apel Capital & Trust Limited “AC&T” a company incorporated in 1992 as a limited liability to render Asset Management, Registrar and Trusteeship services to government and its parastatals, corporate and individual clients.

Apel Capital Registrars Limited has evolved from a one-stop data management to a capital advisory and a provider of high standard technology enabled solutions that have helped our clients in the ever-competitive market.

Over the past years, Apel Capital Registrars has stood the test of time and has significantly grown its operations and service delivery to both our esteemed client companies and partners by utilizing market opportunities in the interest of all their stakeholders.
